Our Services

Fire Fighting Systems

We provide complete design, installation, testing, and commissioning of fire fighting systems in compliance with UAE Civil Defence standards. Our solutions include sprinkler systems, hydrant systems, hose reel systems, and fire pumps for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.

Fire Alarm Systems

Our fire alarm systems are designed to detect fire hazards at an early stage and alert occupants immediately. We install conventional and addressable fire alarm systems, smoke detectors, heat detectors, and control panels ensuring maximum safety and reliability.

Fire Suppression Systems

We offer advanced fire suppression solutions including FM200, CO2, and clean agent systems suitable for server rooms, data centers, and high-risk areas. Our systems ensure fast response and minimal damage to equipment and property.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

We provide comprehensive AMC services to ensure all fire safety systems remain fully operational. Our maintenance includes routine inspections, testing, servicing, and compliance reporting as per UAE safety regulations.

Emergency & CCTV Systems

We install emergency lighting systems and CCTV surveillance solutions to enhance safety and security. Our systems are designed to provide visibility during emergencies and continuous monitoring for commercial and residential properties.

Fire Extinguisher Refilling

We provide refilling, servicing, and certification for all types of fire extinguishers including CO2, foam, dry powder, and water-based extinguishers. Our services ensure compliance with safety standards and readiness during emergencies.

LPG Systems

Our LPG system services include design, installation, and maintenance of centralized gas systems for restaurants, hotels, and residential buildings, ensuring safety and efficiency.

Electromechanical Services

We provide complete electromechanical (MEP) solutions including installation and maintenance of electrical, plumbing, and mechanical systems for commercial and industrial projects.

About Us

Siri Seven Fire Safety Equipment Installation L.L.C is a trusted provider of complete fire protection and safety solutions based in Dubai, UAE. We specialize in the design, installation, testing, commissioning, and maintenance of fire safety systems for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Our expertise includes fire alarm systems, fire fighting systems, LPG central gas systems, fire suppression solutions, and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC). All our services are carried out in compliance with UAE Civil Defence regulations and international safety standards, ensuring maximum protection and reliability.

With a team of skilled technicians and experienced engineers, we are committed to delivering high-quality workmanship, fast response times, and cost-effective solutions tailored to client requirements. Our goal is to ensure the safety of people, property, and assets through reliable and efficient fire protection systems.

We have successfully supported businesses across Dubai and the UAE by providing dependable fire safety services and long-term maintenance solutions. At Siri Seven, safety is not just a service — it is our responsibility.
